THE MARCHÉ NOIR

Marché Noir

is a global creative collective uniting visionary artists, designers, and innovators across the entire creative spectrum. Born in the vibrant hubs of New York City and Stockholm, we are not an agency—we are a dynamic network of creatives collaborating across borders to tackle diverse briefs and deliver extraordinary outcomes. From video and animation to tech, design, and branding, our collective thrives on the belief that creativity knows no boundaries, and by connecting passionate minds worldwide, we craft solutions that are as unique as the challenges we face. At Marché Noir, we bring together diverse talents—filmmakers, animators, UI/UX designers, brand strategists, developers, and storytellers—to create work that resonates. Our decentralized approach allows us to tap into a global pool of expertise, ensuring every project is met with fresh perspectives and cutting-edge ideas. Whether it’s a captivating motion graphic, an immersive digital interface, a transformative brand identity, or a groundbreaking tech solution, we’re here to make it happen, together.

Our Mission

We exist to redefine how creative work is done. Marché Noir is a movement that empowers independent creatives to collaborate without constraints, blending artistry with innovation to solve real-world problems. Our mission is to build a borderless network where ideas flow freely, spanning video, animation, design, tech, and beyond, and every brief is an opportunity to push the limits of what’s possible. We’re not just creating for today—we’re shaping the future of creativity.

How We Work

Marché Noir operates as a fluid collective, not a rigid agency. Here’s how we bring ideas to life:
  • Global Collaboration: From NYC to Stockholm and beyond, we assemble tailored teams of creatives for each project, drawing from our growing network to match the right skills to the right challenge.
  • Flexible Approach: No cookie-cutter solutions. We adapt to each brief, combining research, strategy, and creativity to deliver results that are both impactful and authentic.
  • Diverse Expertise: Our collective spans the full creative spectrum—video production, animation, UI/UX design, branding, tech development, and more—ensuring we can tackle any creative challenge with precision and flair.
  • Open Invitation: We’re always looking for passionate creatives to join our network. Whether you’re an animator in Tokyo, a filmmaker in São Paulo, a designer in London, or a developer anywhere else, Marché Noir is a place to collaborate and grow.
Ready to create something extraordinary? Let’s connect and bring your vision to life.
